In-depth News: Urban Life 深度报道：城市生活实录

A 3-month in-depth investigation on the living conditions of urban grassroots groups 历时3个月的城市草根群体生存状态深度调查

Project Overview 项目概述

This in-depth news project focuses on the living conditions, challenges and aspirations of urban grassroots groups including delivery riders, street vendors, and community cleaners. Through 50+ interviews and on-site investigations in 8 districts of the city, the project presents a true and comprehensive picture of the survival status of this group. 本次深度报道项目聚焦外卖骑手、街头小贩、社区保洁等城市草根群体的生存状态、面临的挑战与诉求。通过对城市8个行政区的50+人次访谈和实地调研，真实、全面地呈现了该群体的生存现状。

Key Findings 核心发现

  • 78% of delivery riders work more than 10 hours a day, with an average monthly income of 5000-8000 RMB78%的外卖骑手日均工作超10小时，月均收入5000-8000元
  • Street vendors face frequent administrative penalties, but 90% of them have no other source of income街头小贩频繁面临行政处罚，但90%的小贩无其他收入来源
  • Community cleaners have an average age of 58, with no social security coverage in 65% of cases社区保洁人员平均年龄58岁，65%无社保覆盖

Publication & Influence 刊发与影响

The report was published in 3 core media platforms, with a total reading volume of over 1 million, and attracted attention from relevant government departments, which subsequently introduced targeted improvement measures for the rights and interests protection of grassroots groups. 该报道刊发于3家核心媒体平台，总阅读量超100万，引发相关政府部门关注，后续出台了针对草根群体权益保障的专项改进措施。

Photo Series: Rural Memory 摄影系列：乡村记忆

Recording the changes of rural areas in 5 representative cities of China 记录中国5个代表性城市的乡村变迁

香港 · Hong Kong

Rural memory photography series in Hong Kong, recording the traditional villages and cultural heritage in the New Territories. This series includes 12 works, focusing on the Hakka villages in the New Territories and the changes of fishing villages in Lantao Island. 香港乡村记忆摄影系列，记录新界地区的传统村落与文化遗产。本系列包含12幅作品，聚焦新界客家村落及大屿山渔村变迁。

青岛 · Qingdao

Coastal rural culture in Qingdao, focusing on the fishing village culture and marine heritage. 15 works record the changes of fishing villages along the coast of Jiaozhou Bay over the past 10 years. 青岛沿海乡村文化，聚焦渔村文化与海洋遗产。15幅作品记录胶州湾沿岸渔村近10年的变迁。

西安 · Xi'an

Ancient rural culture in the Guanzhong Plain, recording the traditional farming civilization. 10 works show the farming customs and folk culture of the ancient villages in Zhouzhi County. 关中平原古村落文化，记录传统农耕文明。10幅作品展现周至县古村落的农耕习俗与民俗文化。

南京 · Nanjing

Water town culture in southern Jiangsu, focusing on the rural landscape of the Yangtze River Delta. 18 works present the water town style and rural revitalization achievements of Gaochun District. 苏南水乡文化，聚焦长三角乡村风貌。18幅作品呈现高淳区的水乡风貌与乡村振兴成果。

台湾 · Taiwan

Cross-strait rural cultural exchanges, recording the traditional rural customs of Taiwan. 14 works show the rural style of Tainan and Hualien, reflecting the cultural identity between cross-strait rural areas. 两岸乡村文化交流，记录台湾传统乡村风貌。14幅作品展现台南、花莲的乡村风貌，体现两岸乡村的文化同根性。

Short Film: The River 短片作品：河流

A 15-minute documentary short film about the ecological protection of urban rivers 15分钟城市河流生态保护纪实短片

Film Introduction 影片介绍

"The River" is a 15-minute documentary short film that records the ecological restoration process of an urban river over the past 5 years. The film uses a follow-up shooting method to present the changes of the river from a stinking ditch to a clear waterway through the perspectives of environmental protection volunteers, river management personnel and nearby residents. 《河流》是一部15分钟的纪实短片，记录了一条城市河流近5年的生态修复过程。影片采用跟踪拍摄的方式，通过环保志愿者、河道管理人员、周边居民等视角，呈现了这条河流从臭水沟到清水廊道的变迁。

Production Information 制作信息

Duration:时长： 15 minutes 28 seconds
Shooting Period:拍摄周期： 8 months (2022-2023)
Shooting Equipment:拍摄设备： Sony FX3 + DJI Air 2S
Post-production:后期制作： Adobe Premiere Pro + DaVinci Resolve
