More than 40% of Hong Kong's "Elderly caring for the elderly" family caregivers suffer from depression and anxiety 香港逾四成“以老护老”家庭照顾者受抑郁焦虑困扰

By Vicky Sun作者：Vicky Sun Oct 13, 20252025年10月13日

A recent study on family caregivers in Hong Kong has revealed that the mental health issues among the city's "elderly caring for the elderly" demographic require urgent attention. According to a survey released by the Hong Kong Polytechnic University in August 2025, over 40% of the 2,000 family caregivers interviewed faced mental health issues, with approximately 1,000 showing symptoms of depression and 500 exhibiting symptoms of anxiety. The survey also found that around 1,200 respondents belonged to the "elderly caring for the elderly" group, where elderly individuals care for even older or disabled family members.香港理工大学于2025年8月发布的一项调查显示，在接受访问的2000名家庭照顾者中，超过40%面临心理健康问题，其中约1000人出现抑郁症状，500人出现焦虑症状。调查还发现，约1200名受访者属于“以老护老”群体，即由长者照顾更年长或残疾的家庭成员。

Wong Kwai-kwong, now 75, is one of the many "elderly-on-elderly caregivers" in Hong Kong. He lives with his 78-year-old wife in a public housing estate in Sha Tin. His wife has been bedridden due to a stroke for twelve years. Wong's daily caregiving tasks include helping her turn over, cleaning, feeding, and more, with an average caregiving time exceeding 16 hours per day.现年75岁的黄贵光（音译）便是香港众多“以老护老”照顾者之一。他与78岁的妻子同住于沙田一个公屋单位。其妻因中风已卧床长达十二年。黄伯每日的护理工作包括帮妻子翻身、清洁、喂食等，日均照顾时间超过16小时。

"At 6 a.m., I have to help her turn over. At 7:30 a.m., I give her medicine. At 9 a.m., I start massaging her," Wong Kwai-kwong said, showing the care schedule posted on his wall. Next to the schedule are the contact information for the Sha Tin Care Team and some caregiving notes.“早上6点，我要帮她翻身。7点30分，喂她吃药。9点，开始帮她按摩，”黄贵光指着贴在墙上的照顾时间表说。时间表旁边贴着沙田关爱队的联络方式和一些护理备忘。

Hong Kong charity "Suicide Prevention Services" released the findings of a survey on carer stress in April 2025. The study, conducted between October of the previous year and February of the current year, surveyed a total of 942 carers. Results showed that 37.4% of respondents were in a "high-load" state. Among these high-load carers, 40.3% simultaneously exhibited symptoms of depression or anxiety. The survey also revealed that working carers and those with excessively long weekly caregiving hours faced greater pressure and risks.香港慈善机构“生命热线”于2025年4月发布了照顾者压力调查结果。该研究于去年10月至今年2月进行，共访问了942名照顾者。结果显示，37.4%的受访者处于“高压”状态。在这些高压照顾者中，40.3%同时出现抑郁或焦虑症状。调查还显示，在职照顾者及每周照顾时间过长的人士面临更大的压力和风险。

Lam Yuk-wah, the volunteer general secretary of the Gracious Love Society in Sha Tin District, said, "In the cases we encounter, many caregivers are already in their seventies or eighties and still need to care for even older spouses or disabled family members. Due to traditional beliefs, some elderly individuals are reluctant to seek external help, leading to long-term emotional suppression. I remember one time Mr. Wong had a fever but was afraid to see a doctor because he worried no one would care for his wife."沙田区“感恩关爱社”的义务总干事林玉华（音译）表示：“在我们接触的个案中，不少照顾者本身已年过七、八十岁，仍要照顾更年长的配偶或残疾家人。由于传统观念，部分长者不愿寻求外界协助，导致情绪长期受压。我记得有一次黄伯发烧，但不敢去看医生，因为他担心没人照顾妻子。”

In response to this social issue, the Hong Kong Social Welfare Department announced that starting July 2025, it would establish a reporting mechanism with the Hospital Authority to ensure caregivers can provide timely assistance when those they care for are hospitalized. The "Care Team Support for the Elderly and Caregivers Program" has been extended to all 18 districts in Hong Kong, expected to cover over 60,000 families in need.针对这一社会问题，香港社会福利署宣布，自2025年7月起，将与医院管理局建立通报机制，确保照顾者能在被照顾者住院时获得及时协助。“长者及照顾者关爱队支援计划”已扩展至全港18区，预计将覆盖超过6万个有需要家庭。

Since expanding its services in 2024, the Tai Wai Jockey Club Caregiver Support Centre has provided support to over 500 caregiver families. The services offered by the centre include up to 8 hours of respite care per week, professional nursing training, caregiver mutual support groups, and psychological counseling.大围赛马会照顾者支援中心自2024年扩展服务以来，已为超过500个照顾者家庭提供支援。中心提供的服务包括每周最多8小时的暂托服务、专业护理培训、照顾者互助小组及心理辅导。

Through a referral from the Sha Tin Care Team at the end of 2024, Wong Kwai-kwong began receiving services from the Tai Wai Jockey Club Caregiver Center. The centre provided him with 4 hours of free respite care per week.透过沙田关爱队于2024年底的转介，黄贵光开始接受大围赛马会照顾者中心的服务。中心为他提供每周4小时的免费暂托服务。

"Respite care allows caregivers to have short breaks," said Ma Hoi-yan, the program manager of the Tai Wai Jockey Club Caregiver Center. "Some caregivers leave their family members here for two hours to go out and get some fresh air, which makes them feel much better psychologically."“暂托服务让照顾者能有短暂休息，”大围赛马会照顾者中心项目经理马海欣（音译）表示。“有些照顾者会将家人送来两小时，自己出去透透气，心理上会舒服很多。”

Now, what Mr. Wong looks forward to most each week is the home visits from the Care Team. The volunteers not only bring supplies but, more importantly, chat with him, allowing him to temporarily set down his burdens. The respite care provided by the Tai Wai Jockey Club Caregiver Support Centre has given him rare breathing space.如今，黄伯每星期最期待的就是关爱队的探访。义工们不仅带来物资，更重要的是陪他聊天，让他能暂时放下重担。大围赛马会照顾者支援中心提供的暂托服务，给了他难得的喘息空间。

In front of the serving window, gray-haired seniors line up quietly with trays, the clink of stainless steel plates sounding soft and clear.上午11点半，罗湖区翠竹街道长者服务中心的食堂里飘出温暖而混合的香气：清蒸鱼、浓烧豆腐、清炒时蔬。打菜窗口前，银发长者们端着餐盘安静排队，不锈钢餐盘碰撞的声音清脆而柔和。

Li Xia, 78, leans forward slightly and picks carefully from several gentle, steaming soft dishes. A standard lunch here costs 18 yuan for three dishes and one soup, chosen from six daily fresh options. With Shenzhen's senior meal subsidy, Li only needs to tap her card and pay 13 yuan for a full, warm, nutritious meal.78岁的李霞微微前倾，仔细挑选了几道温热软烂的菜肴。这里标准午餐是18元，三菜一汤，每日六种新鲜菜品可选。加上深圳市长者助餐补贴，李霞只需刷卡支付13元，就能吃到一顿丰盛、温暖、营养的饭菜。

"Cooking alone at home used to be tiring and lonely," Li said, sitting down with her tray. "Now the food here is safe and tasty, and I can chat with friends while I eat. It's really lively."“以前一个人在家做饭，又累又孤单，”李霞端着餐盘坐下说道。“现在这里的饭菜安全可口，吃饭时还能和老朋友聊聊天，真的很热闹。”

Shenzhen's community senior canteens are no longer just places to fill stomachs. They are a creative space for age-friendly soft food and an emotional home for older residents. With low-oil, low-salt, and tender dishes, the canteens fit the physical needs of seniors with weaker chewing and digestion. Warm, home-style meals also build close emotional bonds: local empty-nesters find company, and migrant seniors feel they belong in their new city.深圳的社区长者食堂已不再是单纯的果腹之处。它们是适老化软食的创新空间，也是老年居民的情感家园。低油低盐、软烂适口的菜品，贴合了咀嚼和消化能力较弱的老年人的身体需求。温暖的家常菜也建立了紧密的情感纽带：本地空巢老人找到了陪伴，随迁长者在新城市找到了归属感。

This canteen serves two main groups: more than 20 seniors living long-term in the senior apartment and nearby community residents with official permits. Sometimes students and office workers nearby also join the seniors for the affordable, clean food. The kitchen uses only steaming, stewing, and braising — gentle cooking methods for seniors. Several large steamers work from early morning to make sure every dish is fresh and soft.这家食堂主要服务两类人群：长期居住在长者公寓的20多位老人，以及持有正式许可的周边社区居民。有时附近的学生和上班族也会前来与老人一起享用实惠洁净的饭菜。厨房采用蒸、炖、烧等适合老年人的温和烹饪方式。几台大型蒸锅从清晨就开始工作，确保每一道菜品都新鲜软烂。

Chen Xiulian, 76, was born and raised in Shenzhen and lives alone after her husband died. With bad teeth, she comes to the canteen almost every weekday and sits in her usual window seat. "Eating here is so easy," Chen said. "The dishes are soft, not salty or oily, and good for my health." She lifted a piece of tender steamed sole easily with her chopsticks; the fish was so soft it slipped down her throat with almost no chewing.76岁的陈秀莲是土生土长的深圳人，丈夫去世后独居。因牙齿不好，她几乎每个工作日都来食堂，坐在她惯常的靠窗位置。“在这里吃饭很方便，”陈说。“菜品软烂，不咸不油，对我的健康有好处。”她用筷子轻松夹起一块嫩滑的清蒸龙利鱼，鱼肉软糯得几乎无需咀嚼就滑入喉咙。

Yang Mingfan, 82, sitting next to Chen, felt the same. He spooned some soft braised wax gourd and said: "Eating at home alone is quiet and sad. Here we talk about old days and our grandchildren. It's warm and busy, and new people become friends quickly." A senior from another table came over with a tea cup, and Yang moved over quietly to make space.坐在陈秀莲旁边的82岁杨明凡深有同感。他舀了一勺软烂的焖冬瓜说：“一个人在家吃饭冷冷清清，很不是滋味。在这里大家聊聊往事，说说孙辈，又热闹又温暖，新朋友很快就熟了。”邻桌一位老人端着茶杯过来，杨明凡默默地挪了挪位置让出空间。

Zhang Weiqiang, 45, head chef of the canteen, stood by the half-open kitchen window and watched the busy dining room. He said his team changes all dishes to fit seniors: less oil and salt, no frying, and softer vegetables. These small changes help seniors with high blood pressure, diabetes and other long-term illnesses. "We don't just make food to eat," Zhang said. "We make food that lets seniors eat safely and peacefully."45岁的食堂主厨张伟强站在半开放式厨房窗口，望着忙碌的餐厅。他说团队对菜品进行了全面适老化调整：少油少盐、不油炸、蔬菜更软烂。这些细微改变帮助患有高血压、糖尿病等慢性病的老人更好地管理健康。“我们不只是在做一顿饭，”张伟强说。“我们在做让老人吃得安心、吃得放心的饭。”

Not far from Cuizhu, Lixin Community Senior Canteen in Dongmen Street is just as lively. Before 11 a.m. every day, seniors arrive with cloth bags and walking sticks. Many of them are migrant seniors who moved to Shenzhen to live with their children.距翠竹不远的东门街道立新社区长者食堂同样热闹。每天上午11点前，老人们便带着布袋和拐杖陆续到来。其中不少是来深圳随迁养老的长者。

The canteen changes home-style dishes to fit different hometown tastes while making every bite soft and easy to chew. During traditional festivals, workers make light, healthy festival food: low-sugar Cantonese mooncakes and soft glutinous rice dumplings. These small treats let seniors enjoy festival traditions in a healthy way.食堂将家常菜调整为适合不同家乡口味的同时，确保每一口都软糯易嚼。传统节日时，工作人员会制作清淡健康的节日食品：低糖广式月饼、软糯汤圆。这些小吃让老人在健康的前提下享受节日传统。

Wang Guixiang, 72, came to Shenzhen from Hunan three years ago. Her son's family likes light Cantonese food with little spice, and her loose teeth cannot handle hard meat. "I missed Hunan braised pork so much," Wang said. "I ate it every week back home, but I couldn't have it here until now. The chef cooks it very soft and easy to chew. It's not very spicy, but the meat taste is just like home." She lifted a shaking piece of red braised pork with her chopsticks.72岁的王桂香三年前从湖南来到深圳。儿子一家喜欢清淡少辣的粤菜，她松动的牙齿也咬不动硬肉。“我太想念湖南红烧肉了，”王桂香说。“在老家我每周都吃，来深圳后一直吃不到。现在厨师把它烧得特别软烂，入口即化。虽然不太辣，但肉香就是家里的味道。”她用筷子夹起一块颤巍巍的红烧肉。

Even better, Wang met three other women from Hunan here. Every lunchtime, the four sit together. They talk over soft steamed spareribs, braised eggplant and wax gourd soup — about expensive hometown vegetables in Shenzhen markets, and when to smoke cured meat back home.更妙的是，王桂香在这里认识了三个湖南老乡。每天午饭时间，四人坐在一起，吃着软糯的蒸排骨、烧茄子和冬瓜汤，聊着深圳菜市场里老家的菜有多贵，聊着什么时候回老家熏腊肉。

"At first I just came for cheap food," Wang said. "Now this is my favorite social time every day. We talk about small things: market prices, grandchildren's homework, weather back home. These talks make me feel connected and not lonely."“一开始我只是图便宜，”王桂香说。“现在这成了我每天最期待的社交时光。我们聊些小事：菜价、孙子的作业、老家的天气。这些闲聊让我感到与故乡的联结，不再孤单。”

The strong system of Shenzhen's senior canteens depends on government support. According to the Citywide Senior Meal Service Construction Report released by the Shenzhen Municipal Bureau of Civil Affairs in 2025, the city had built 266 standardized senior canteens and meal service spots by June 2025. In 2024, the service provided more than 127,000 meals and helped 200,000 seniors with financial subsidies.深圳长者食堂的强大体系离不开政府支持。根据深圳市民政局2025年发布的《全市长者助餐服务建设报告》，截至2025年6月，全市已建成266个标准化长者食堂和助餐点。2024年，该服务提供了超过12.7万份餐食，并为20万长者提供了财政补贴。

The subsidy rules follow the Shenzhen Elderly Meal Subsidy Implementation Rules revised in 2024: Shenzhen registered seniors aged 70 to 99 get up to 5 yuan per meal; registered low-income, disabled and bereaved seniors aged 60 and above get up to 15 yuan per meal. Seniors can apply easily with their ID card and household registration book at their community office. The government also gives about 2 yuan per meal to support canteen operations.补贴规则依据2024年修订的《深圳市长者助餐补贴实施办法》：70至99岁深圳户籍长者每餐最高补贴5元；60岁以上户籍低保、残疾及失独长者每餐最高补贴15元。长者凭身份证和户口本即可在社区工作站轻松申请。政府还给予每餐约2元的运营补贴支持食堂运转。

In fast-paced Shenzhen, many adult children work long hours, leaving seniors at risk of loneliness and poor nutrition. Senior canteens offer more than soft, healthy food — they offer a warm, social space. All canteens follow the Nutrition Meals Guideline for the Elderly, a local Shenzhen standard that took effect in February 2025. Some canteens also invite nutritionists to help design meals for seniors with long-term illnesses.在快节奏的深圳，许多成年子女长时间工作，老年人面临孤独和营养不足的风险。长者食堂提供的不仅是软烂健康的食物——更是一个温暖的社交空间。所有食堂都遵循2025年2月生效的深圳地方标准《老年人营养餐指南》。部分食堂还邀请营养师协助为患有慢性病的老人设计餐食。

Although some problems remain — such as uneven coverage and trouble for some seniors to apply for subsidies — Shenzhen is improving its services. Lin Ruifan, 42, Member of the Party Working Committee and Director of the Public Service Office of Dongmen Subdistrict, said the city is working to expand and improve the system. "We are building smart dining platforms and testing cross-district subsidies. We want to bring elderly care services to the last mile."尽管仍存在一些问题——如覆盖不均、部分老人申请补贴困难——深圳正在不断改善服务。42岁的东门街道党工委委员、公共服务办公室主任林瑞凡表示，深圳正努力扩大和完善这一体系。“我们正在建设智慧助餐平台，试点跨区补贴。我们要把养老服务送到‘最后一公里’。”

Sweet Basil Thai Cuisine 甜香泰味：香港海港城Sweet Basil Thai Cuisine食评

By Vicky Sun作者：Vicky Sun Mar 15, 20262026年3月15日

I have little exposure to Thai cuisine in daily life, so I specifically chose Sweet Basil Thai Cuisine, a long-established Thai restaurant founded in 1994 at Harbour City in Hong Kong, for my first formal taste of authentic Thai food. Nestled on the second floor of Ocean Terminal, this restaurant is quite popular in Tsim Sha Tsui thanks to its authentic Thai decor and stunning views of Victoria Harbour. That said, my dining experience here left me feeling a touch disappointed overall.我平日很少接触泰国菜，所以这次特意选择了香港海港城这家创立于1994年的老牌泰餐厅Sweet Basil Thai Cuisine，作为我第一次正式品尝地道泰餐的起点。餐厅位于海运大厦二楼，凭借地道的泰式装潢和维港美景，在尖沙咀颇受欢迎。不过，整体用餐体验让我略感失望。

Stepping into the restaurant, I was immediately enveloped in a strong exotic Thai atmosphere. The interior is exquisitely and luxuriously decorated, adorned with Thai ornaments everywhere and traditional patterns on the walls, standing distinctly apart from minimalist-style Thai eateries. The space is well laid out: window-side seats offer an unobstructed view of Victoria Harbour, and there are large private rooms available, perfect for gatherings with friends or business dinners. The waitstaff are dressed in uniform, gentle in manner and soft-spoken, and they are extremely prompt at changing plates and refilling water. A 10% service charge is applied at the restaurant, and coupons are provided upon checkout—a thoughtful little detail.踏入餐厅，浓郁的异国泰式风情扑面而来。内部装潢精致奢华，随处可见泰国饰品，墙面装饰着传统图案，与简约风格的泰式餐厅截然不同。空间布局合理：靠窗座位可一览维港美景，还有大型包间可供选择，适合朋友聚会或商务宴请。服务员穿着统一制服，举止温婉、轻声细语，更换餐盘和添水非常及时。餐厅收取10%服务费，结账时提供优惠券——一个贴心的细节。

We both ordered the lunch set meal, which includes one appetizer, one main course and one drink per person, with clear labels on the menu for each option. The highlight of the meal was undoubtedly the Iced Longan Drink. It contains about seven shelled longans with plump, firm flesh; on the palate, it is sweet but not cloying, with a natural fruity aroma, and also boasts nourishing properties. Its icy, smooth texture awakens the taste buds in an instant, making it an ideal pre-meal drink. The Iced Thai Milk Tea was also a pleasant surprise: free from the usual cloying sweetness, it has a mild, delicate flavor, with milkiness and the distinct aroma of hand-labeled black tea blending harmoniously. The tea flavor is rich and mellow, offering a refreshing, non-sugary taste. Fried Flat Noodles with Soy Sauce (Pork) is another standout dish. The flat noodles are stir-fried to a glossy, non-sticky finish with a satisfying chewy texture, and the pork slices are tender and well-infused with sauce. Stir-fried together with eggs, celery and green vegetables, it has a rich wok hei and an enticing aroma. The only minor flaw lies in the side vegetables: the green vegetables are crisp and tender but carry a slight bitter note, and the carrots, cut into an elegant lace pattern for a refined look, lack flavor infusion, which is a pity.我们两人都点了午市套餐，每人包含一份前菜、一份主菜和一杯饮品，菜单上对每个选项都有清晰标注。整顿饭的亮点无疑是冰镇龙眼水。里面大约有七颗去壳龙眼，果肉饱满紧实；入口清甜不腻，带有天然果香，还有滋补功效。冰爽顺滑的口感瞬间唤醒味蕾，是绝佳的开胃饮品。冰泰式奶茶也令人惊喜：没有常见的甜腻感，味道柔和细腻，奶香与手标红茶的独特香气和谐交融。茶香浓郁醇厚，口感清爽不甜腻。酱油炒河粉（猪肉）是另一道出色菜品。河粉炒得油亮不粘，口感Q弹有嚼劲，猪肉片嫩滑入味。与鸡蛋、西芹和青菜同炒，锅气十足，香气诱人。唯一的瑕疵是配菜：青菜脆嫩但带一丝微苦，胡萝卜切成精致的蕾丝花边，外形优雅却不够入味，实属遗憾。

The biggest letdown of the meal was the Fried Rice with Seafood and Pineapple. I had high expectations for this classic Thai dish, yet it fell far short of what I anticipated. Served in a half pineapple whose flesh was dry and withered, the fruit was clearly not fresh, having been stored for too long. The squid was pleasantly chewy, but the crab sticks were mushy and fell apart at the slightest touch, completely devoid of flavor. The corn, green peas and carrots in the fried rice had a stiff, raw texture, presumably frozen ingredients that were stir-fried without being fully thawed. Furthermore, the fried rice was overpowered by a strong pepper taste, which masked the umami of the seafood and the natural sweetness of the pineapple. Instead of a harmonious fusion of flavors, it felt like a haphazard mix of ingredients simply tossed together in a pan. Pineapple contains bromelain, an enzyme that tenderizes meat, yet I detected no hint of the subtle, blended texture between the pineapple and the meat in this dish whatsoever.整顿饭最大的失望是菠萝海鲜炒饭。我对这道经典泰式菜肴期望很高，但它远未达到预期。盛放在半个菠萝中，果肉干瘪枯萎，明显不新鲜，存放时间过长。鱿鱼有嚼劲，但蟹棒糊烂松散，一碰就碎，毫无味道。炒饭中的玉米粒、青豆和胡萝卜口感生硬，估计是冷冻食材未完全解冻就下锅翻炒。此外，炒饭被浓重的胡椒味盖过，掩盖了海鲜的鲜味和菠萝的自然清甜。本应是风味的和谐融合，吃起来却像食材被随意扔进锅里翻炒。菠萝含有菠萝蛋白酶，能嫩化肉质，但我在这道菜中完全没有感受到菠萝与肉之间那种微妙的融合口感。

Deep Fried Crunchy Chicken Bones was a solid hit: crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, the chicken bones were crunchy but not hard on the teeth. The delicate fragrance of perilla leaves cut through the greasiness of the fried dish, and the sweet and spicy sauce added rich layers of flavor, making it more delicious with every bite. In stark contrast, the Deep Fried Shrimp Cakes were underwhelming. The shrimp paste had been over-whisked, robbing the cakes of the springy, chewy texture fresh shrimp should bring, leaving them loose and crumbly. Even paired with plum sauce, they failed to redeem their lackluster flavor.酥炸脆鸡骨是扎实的亮点：外酥里嫩，鸡骨酥脆而不硌牙。紫苏叶的清香中和了油炸的油腻，甜辣酱增添了丰富的层次感，越吃越香。相比之下，炸虾饼令人失望。虾胶搅打过度，失去了鲜虾应有的弹牙口感，变得松散易碎。即使搭配梅子酱，也无法拯救其平淡无味的表现。

All in all, this long-established Thai restaurant with nearly 30 years of history has distinct strengths and weaknesses. Its top-notch service, immersive Thai-themed ambiance, prime location with Victoria Harbour views, and a few standout dishes make it worth a casual visit for the experience. However, the restaurant also has notable shortcomings: some ingredients lack freshness, the flavor combinations are disjointed, and the a la carte prices are on the steep side.总体而言，这家拥有近30年历史的老牌泰餐厅优缺点分明。一流的服务、沉浸式的泰式氛围、坐拥维港美景的优越位置，以及几道出色的菜品，让它值得为体验而一试。不过，餐厅也存在明显短板：部分食材不够新鲜、风味搭配脱节、单点价格偏高。
